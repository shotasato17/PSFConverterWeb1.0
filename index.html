Frequently Asked Questions

Is PSF Converter Free?
Yes, PSF Converter is free for all users. All users will be able to use our latest model and have access to the real-time exchange rates.
Can I use psfconverter.com for currency conversion too?
Yes. The site includes real-time currency exchange rates, so you can easily convert prices from dollars to yen and vice versa.
How do I convert from USD/ft² to JPY/tsubo?
To convert from USD/ft² to JPY/tsubo, follow these steps:
  • Select "USD" as the currency and "sqft" as the metric.
  • Enter the price in USD/ft² in the conversion input field.
  • Select "JPY" as the target currency and "tsubo" as the target metric.
  • The result will be calculated automatically based on real-time exchange rates and the conversion facto.
What is a "tsubo"?
A tsubo is a Japanese unit of area equal to about 3.3 square meters or 35.6 square feet. It’s commonly used in Japanese real estate.
